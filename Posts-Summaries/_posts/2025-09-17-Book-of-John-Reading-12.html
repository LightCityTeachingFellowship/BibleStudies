Book of John (Reading & Study 12)

  1. Jesus said “Unless I wash you, you have no part with me” [Joh 13:8].
    1. To belong to Jesus, one has to be washed.
    2. Even after being clean, one still needs to be washed
  2. Although it isn’t wrong to do so, Jesus doesn’t mean His disciples should literally wash each other’s feet.
  3. Jesus’ knowledge is meant to be proof that He’s who He claims to be [Joh 13:19].
  4. Jesus hid Himself when He perceived danger. We should therefore not be reckless.
